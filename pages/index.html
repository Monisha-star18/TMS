About TravelSync

TravelSync is a modern employee travel request management system designed to simplify business travel across your organisation. Employees submit requests in minutes, managers approve with a single click, and the finance team gets instant visibility into travel spend — all from one clean dashboard.

Built for Indian enterprises, TravelSync supports multi-department workflows, project-based travel tracking, and real-time status updates so nothing falls through the cracks.
